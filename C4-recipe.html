Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour,sugar,baking powder, and salt.
  2. In an another bowl, beat egg with milk and melted bytter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
